Practices

In addition to switching to green energy, there are practices that you can do to help reduce your footprint.

Keep the Lights Off

Don’t leave that light in the kitchen on if you aren't using it. Shut it off so you reduce your energy bill and the carbon footprint that you leave behind.

Drop the Temperature

Bring out your jacket and lower the thermostat a few degrees. Not only are you reducing your carbon footprint even more than you were prior, your energy bill will be thanking you in the long run.

Energy Star is No Joke

When you buy yourself new electronic products, such as appliances, look out for energy efficiency ratings. Things like the blue Energy Star certification can let you know that the product you are looking at doesn’t use energy that much, saving the environment some more.
