Cost Information

Solar Cost

When it comes to solar panel pricing, the average cost for solar panel installation in the U.S. is $16,000.

This cost can vary wildly across the states ranging from below $5,000 soaring above $36,000, so look at your local pricing to makr your decision.

Wind Energy Cost

You could also take a route and get yourself a small wind energy system. This is much more expensive ranging from $10,000 to $70,000, for much more power from 5 kilowatts to 30 kilowatts.

Minimizing Costs

A common way that those with these installations minimize cost is by selling the energy back. When you produce excess energy, you can return it to the energy grid and get paid back.

There also are various incentives that are offered that can help with the cost that you pay.
