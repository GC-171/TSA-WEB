Go Green!

With the rise of climate change, now more than ever do we as people need to change into greener ways of energy.

Thankfully, there are programs for consumers to make this easier as well as common practices that we can can all implement into our daily lives.

General Information FAQ

What is Green Energy?
Green energy are energy solutions that don't harm the environment.
Why Should I switch to Green Energy?
Climate change has become an increasingly bigger problem over the last 2 decades and we need to slow it down however we can.
What are some Common Energy Methods?
The most common methods that people think of when mentioning green energy are solar as well as wind energy.
